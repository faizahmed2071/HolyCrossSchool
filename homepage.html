About Our School

Holy cross school was founded in 1983,at the invitation and in collaboration with TATA STEEL,West Ghato Division,Ghatotand. It is a Christian minorities school, managed by Sisters of Mercy of the Holy Cross, Hazaribag (A registered Charitable Society, engaged in various kind of educational and philanthropical activities throughout India).

The medium of instruction is English. The School promotes Co-Education and it admits any child irrespective of caste, creed or social Status.
