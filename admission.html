Admission

In the agreement between TISCO (West Bokaro) and Holy cross Institution, Hazaribag, the following points relate to admission policy.

  1. The school is a Christian minority run Institution which in matters of admission abides by the norms of the Jharkhand Christian school constitution and Catholic Diocesan Education Board rules.
  2. Eighty percent of the seats are reserved for the children of TISCO (West Bokaro) employees.
  3. We avail the facilities of the school to the children of economically poorer classes.
  4. The school admits all children irrespective of caste, creed, language, social status or place of origin.
  5. The school will admit those who will respond to the values .it stand for, and who will persevere and benefit from the education offered here.
  6. Application for all admissions are to be made on the prescribed form and according to the schedule announced by the school.
  7. Every candidate for admission must be introduced in person to Principal by one responsible for the child.
  8. Admission for all classes in the school is decided by test, and the suitability of the candidate for the school.
  9. Any pressure on the school authorities for granting admission will disqualify the candidate.
  10. The parents who wish their children to be admitted in this school are expected to have an understanding of, and the ability and willingness to cooperate in, the type of education offered here.

Admission to preparatory classes

Application forms are issued in the month of January.

  1. The candidates should be between 4½ and 5½ years of age on 1st April of the year of admission.
  2. The admission process includes interviews during which verification of documents and identification of candidates and parents are done.
  3. Parents must declare the child's correct date of birth in the admission form and must understand that this date cannot be changed at any stage.
  4. Legally valid birth certificate must be attached with the form.
  5. Incorrect declarations about age or any other matter will be a disqualification for admission.
  6. Subsequent discovery by the school authorities of such declaration could be a good ground for dismissal.

Withdrawal

Application for the withdrawal of the child is to be given fifteen days in advance with signature of the parent and stating the cause of withdrawal.

Guardians are advised to meet the Principal in person if a child is to be withdrawn from the school. Transfer certificate will be issued on full payment of the dues and 3-5 days after the application for the same is submitted.

Admmission to other classes (I - IX)

  1. Application for admissions to other classes in the school will be entertained only if there are vacancies. Such admissions will be decided by the selection committee. Tests in main subjects and his/her general suitability for the school will form the basis of this selection.
  2. The selected candidates are admitted on remitting the prescribed fees and on producing their Transfer certificate, and progress report issued by a recognized school.
  3. Documents required for admission becomes the property of the school and shall not be returned on any account.
  4. Requests for admissions to classes X and XII will not be considered.
  5. Those wishing to study in Holy Cross school will be required to apply on the prescribed form, according to the schedule announced by the school.
  6. Candidates seeking admission to Science stream must have a minimum CGPA of 8 points and the aggregate of marks obtained in English, Science Mathematics (excluding practical) should also be not less than 8 points.
  7. Candidates seeking admission to Commerce stream must have a minimum CGPA of 7 points and the aggregate of marks obtained in English, Social Science, Mathematics(excluding practical) should also be not less than 7 points.
  8. Eligibility for admission shall also depend upon regularity in attendance, work and participation in extracurricular and co-curricular activities during previous years.
  9. Character and conduct of the candidates must be to the full satisfaction of the school management. They must furnish a statement regarding satisfactory record of conduct and attitude at the school last attended.
  10. In case of too many candidates applying for one particular stream, some of them may be asked to study another stream. principal's decision is final on this matter.
  11. The following documents must be submitted along with duly filled application form.
    1. Photocopy of mark sheet / provisional mark sheet
    2. Photocopy of certificate of school based evaluation (for students coming from CBSE schools)
    3. Character certificate.
    4. Original Transfer Certificate.
    5. Certificate of co-curricular activities if any.
